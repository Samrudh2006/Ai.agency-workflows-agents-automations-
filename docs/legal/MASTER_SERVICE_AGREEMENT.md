## 1. Scope of Services
**Nexus AI Agency** ("Provider") agrees to provide custom software development, AI agent deployment, and ongoing automation maintenance services to **[Client Company]** ("Client") as detailed in the attached Statement of Work (SOW).

## 2. Term & Termination
This Agreement shall commence on **[Start Date]** and continue until terminated by either party with **thirty (30) days written notice**. Upon termination, Client is responsible for all fees incurred up to the date of termination, and Provider will securely hand over all associated digital assets outlined in the Handoff Document.

## 3. Compensation & Payment Terms
1. **Setup Fees:** A 50% non-refundable deposit is required prior to the commencement of any development work. The remaining 50% is due upon project launch.
2. **Monthly Retainer:** For ongoing API hosting and maintenance, Client agrees to pay **[$Amount]** per month, billed automatically on the 1st of each month via Stripe.
3. **Late Payments:** Invoices unpaid past 15 days will result in a suspension of the AI Agent services until the balance is resolved.

## 4. Intellectual Property
Upon full payment of all setup fees, Client shall own the final deployment, workflows, and prompts integrated into their systems. Provider retains the rights to the underlying, pre-existing proprietary codebase (the "Engine") used to build the automation.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
The Provider utilizes third-party APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Twilio, Make.com) to provide these services. **Provider is not responsible for damages, lost revenue, or system downtime caused by these third-party outages.** While the Provider implements strict prompt guardrails, the inherent nature of LLMs means Provider cannot be held financially liable for minor AI "hallucinations." Our maximum aggregate liability shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the past three (3) months.

## 6. Confidentiality
Provider agrees to hold all Client data, customer lists, and proprietary knowledge bases in strict confidence, implementing "Zero Data Retention" endpoints where applicable to ensure Client data is not used to train public models.
