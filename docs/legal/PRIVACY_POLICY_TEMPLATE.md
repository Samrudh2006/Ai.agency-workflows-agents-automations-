## Nexus AI Agency Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: [Date]**

### 1. Information We Collect
When you interact with our website, book a discovery call, or use our digital services, we may collect the following information:
- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and company name.
- **Usage Data:** IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site.
- **Communication Data:** Transcripts or summaries of conversations you have with our inbound AI Voice Agents or Chatbots.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain our consulting and development services.
- To communicate with you regarding project proposals, updates, and billing.
- To improve our website and agent performance.

### 3. Third-Party Data Processing
As an AI automation agency, we utilize third-party APIs to process data (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Make.com, Vapi.ai). 
**Crucially:** When we process your data through Large Language Models, we strictly utilize Enterprise or Developer API tiers configured for **Zero Data Retention**, meaning your proprietary data is NOT used by these providers to train their public models.

### 4. Data Security
We implement commercially reasonable security measures (including secure environment variables, encrypted databases via Supabase, and HTTPS protocols) to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

### 5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA compliance)
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
To exercise these rights, please contact us at **privacy@nexusaiagency.com**.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
