# 📦 Client Handoff Documentation
**How to document the build so the client (or a future dev) understands it.**

</div>

<br />

Once a project is finished, we provide the client with a secure, professional "Operations Manual." This prevents endless questions asking "how do I change X?"

---

## 📑 Required Documentation Components

Every finished project must include a PDF or protected Notion link containing:

### 1. The Architecture Blueprint
- A simple flowchart diagram showing how their tools are connected. 
- *(e.g. Wix Form → Make.com → ChatGPT → Google Sheets)*

### 2. The "Knowledge Base" Update Guide
- **For Voice/Chat Agents:** Step-by-step instructions on how the client can upload new PDFs/Data to train the bot further.
- **For Automations:** A clear warning: *"Do NOT alter the names of columns in your Google Sheet, or the automation will break."*

### 3. Account Ownership & Vaulted Credentials
For security and liability reasons, the client must own their foundational accounts.
- List all accounts created on their behalf (e.g., Twilio sub-accounts, OpenAI API keys).
- Securely transfer ownership to their admin email.

### 4. Emergency Contacts & SLA
- Re-iterate the terms of their monthly retainer.
- Provide the designated support email (`support@nexusaiagency.com`).

---

## 🎬 The Training Video

Written guides are good, but loom videos are better.
The Account Manager must record a 5-minute screencast demonstrating the finished product live.

**Outline for the video:**
0:00 - Introduction & Congratulations on the launch.
0:30 - Live demo of the Agent taking a call / answering a chat.
2:00 - Showing the result in their CRM.
3:00 - How they can view analytics and call logs.
4:00 - Reminder on how to contact support for the monthly retainer.
