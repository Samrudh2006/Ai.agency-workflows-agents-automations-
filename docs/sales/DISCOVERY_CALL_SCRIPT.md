# 📞 AI Agency Discovery Call Script
**How to diagnose business problems and pitch AI automation**

</div>

<br />

Use this script as a framework for your first Zoom call/Meeting with a prospect. The goal is NOT to talk about AI, but rather to uncover expensive bottlenecks in their business.

---

## 🎯 Phase 1: Rapport & The "Why Now?" (5 mins)

**Goal:** Establish authority and understand why they took the call today.

> "Hi [Name], great to connect. To make sure we make the best use of our time, I have an agenda prepared, but first: **What specifically prompted you to take this call today regarding your current operations?**"

*Listen carefully. Note their exact words.*

## 🔍 Phase 2: The Deep Diagnosis (15 mins)

**Goal:** Find the manual tasks that are bleeding money or stunting growth.

Ask these exact questions:

1. **The Inbound Bottleneck:** "When a new lead comes in (via website/call/FB ad), map out the exact steps your team takes until that lead becomes a paying customer."
2. **The Time Sink:** "If you had to guess, what repetitive task is eating up 10-20 hours of your team’s week right now?"
3. **The Missed Opportunity:** "Are there incoming calls or messages that happen after-hours or on weekends? How are those handled currently?"

## 💡 Phase 3: The "Aha!" Moment & Future Pacing (10 mins)

**Goal:** Show them the cost of inaction and map out the automated future.

> "So what I'm hearing is that currently, every time a lead fills out a form, Sarah has to manually copy that data into HubSpot, then remember to send an email, and half the time leads slip through the cracks. Sound accurate?"

**The Pitch (No-Code Automation):**
> "What if we eliminated that entirely? We connect your website form to a webhook. The moment a lead submits it, an AI instantly qualifies them, updates HubSpot, and sends a hyper-personalized email drafted specifically for their use-case within 5 seconds. How would that impact Sarah's day?"

**The Pitch (Voice Agent):**
> "We build you a custom AI Voice Agent that answers perfectly on the first ring, 24/7. It sounds human, asks your qualifying questions, and books appointments directly onto your calendar. No more missed deals because someone called at 9 PM on a Saturday."

## 💰 Phase 4: Budget & Next Steps (5 mins)

**Goal:** Disqualify cheap clients and set up the proposal meeting.

> "Based on what you've shared, I'm confident we can automate about 60% of that manual work. Normally, a mid-level hire to do this costs what, $40k-$50k a year? We build these permanent, non-sleeping digital employees for a fraction of that."

> "I need to map out the exact technical blueprint for this. If I come back on Thursday with a visual chart showing how the AI connects to your CRM, and the exact ROI you can expect, does [Time] work to review the proposal?"
