# 🧮 Pricing Calculator & Service Tiers
**How to package and price your AI Agency services without undercharging.**

</div>

<br />

Never bill by the hour. Always value-price based on the money you save or generate for the client.

## 💰 The Agency "SaaS + Retainer" Model
The most profitable agencies charge a **Setup Fee** to build the automation, and a **Monthly Retainer** to host, maintain, and provide API credits.

---

### Tier 1: The AI Voice Receptionist
*Perfect for Real Estate, Med-Spas, Lawyers, Plumbers.*

| Deliverable | Description | One-Time Setup | Monthly Retainer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Custom Voice Agent** | Trained on company FAQs and tone. | $1,500 - $3,000 | - |
| **Twilio Integration** | Setup local phone number routing. | Included | - |
| **Calendar Sync** | Books directly to Google Calendar/Calendly. | Included | - |
| **Hosting & API Costs**| We cover up to 500 minutes of AI voice time. | - | $299 - $500/mo |

**ROI Pitch to Client:** "A human receptionist costs $3,500/mo. Our AI costs $500/mo and works 24/7 without taking lunch breaks."

---

## 📈 Upsell Opportunities
Once a client is on a retainer, offer these add-ons 3 months later:
- **Monthly Analytics Review:** A 1-hour call analyzing chat logs to find user pain points (+$250/mo).
- **Multi-lingual Support:** Cloning the Voice agent to speak Spanish/French seamlessly (+$500 setup).
- **SMS Follow-ups:** Adding a Twilio SMS blast workflow after a call ends (+$300 setup).
