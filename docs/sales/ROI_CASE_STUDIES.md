## 📈 Case Study 1: The Real Estate Brokerage

**The Problem:** 
A regional real estate office was missing 15% of inbound calls because agents were out showing homes. Every missed call from a buyer was a potential $10,000 commission lost.

**The Agency Solution:** 
We deployed an **Inbound AI Voice Agent** (using Vapi + Supabase). The agent was programmed to:
1. Answer missed calls immediately.
2. Ask 3 qualifying questions (Budget, Timeline, Pre-approved?).
3. Text a Calendly link to book a showing.

**The ROI:**
- **Missed Calls:** Reduced to 0%.
- **Lead Capture:** 22 new qualified leads captured in Month 1 that would have gone to voicemail.
- **Estimated Revenue Saved:** $40,000+ in potential gross commissions.
- **Agency Profit:** $2,500 setup + $400/mo retainer.

## 📈 Case Study 2: The E-commerce Support Desk

**The Problem:** 
A DTC skincare brand was overwhelmed during Q4. 70% of their Zendesk tickets were simply people asking *"Has my order shipped left?"* Support staff was burning out.

**The Agency Solution:** 
We deployed a **RAG Chatbot Widget**.
1. Connected via API to Shopify.
2. If the user asked about an order, the AI asked for an order number, pinged the API, and returned real-time tracking data.
3. If the user asked for a refund, it routed them to a human.

**The ROI:**
- **Support Deflection:** 68% of total tickets resolved without a human.
- **Cost Savings:** Client paused hiring 2 seasonal temp workers ($8,000 savings).
- **Agency Profit:** $1,500 setup + $350/mo retainer.

## 📈 Case Study 3: The Marketing Agency Lead Flow

**The Problem:** 
A B2B lead generation agency was manually scraping LinkedIn profiles, copy-pasting data into Google Sheets, and having interns write custom first-lines for cold emails.

**The Agency Solution:** 
We built a **No-Code Automation (Make.com)**.
1. Webhook triggers when a new LinkedIn URL is added to Airtable.
2. Scrape API pulls profile data.
3. OpenAI parses the data and writes 3 variations of a hyper-personalized email opener.
4. Data is pushed directly into their Apollo.io campaign.

**The ROI:**
- **Time Saved:** 40 hours per week (1 full-time intern equivalent).
- **Output:** Campaign volume increased by 300%.
- **Agency Profit:** $3,000 custom build + $500/mo API & Maintenance retainer.
